About


The Department offers B. Tech. in Computer Science & Engineering stream. The main focus of the department is to produce graduates with strong basics in Computer Science and Engineering domain. Students are trained to be at the cutting edge of tomorrow’s technologies. The course contains study in Computer Science and its Applications as well as detailed study of various aspects of its working.

Along with B. Tech., Department has started two Post graduate programs in M. Tech. in Cyber Forensics & Information Security and M. Tech. in Data Science. Being focused to research the department has initiated Ph. D. Programs of both Full time & Part time.

The department has qualified faculty members with rich experience in teaching and industry. The faculty members are actively involved in various areas of research. The curriculum provides in-depth knowledge of both hardware and software skills. The curriculum is updated regularly to keep up with the growing demands and changing trends of the software industry. The Department has state-of-the-art infrastructure enabling high quality learning.
